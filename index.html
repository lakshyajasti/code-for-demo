U.S. Government System Notice

You are accessing a U.S. Government (USG) Information System (IS) provided for USG-authorized use only. The USG routinely monitors communications on this IS, may inspect and seize data at any time, and communications are not private. Security measures on this IS protect USG interests — not your personal benefit or privacy. Notwithstanding the above, using this IS does not constitute consent to PM, LE or CI investigative searching or monitoring of privileged communications. See User Agreement for details.

Authority

10 U.S.C. 134, Under Secretary of Defense for Policy; DoDD 5101.1, DoD Executive Agent; DoDD 5105.65, Defense Security Cooperation Agency (DSCA); DoDD 5132.03, DoD Policy and Responsibilities Relating to Security Cooperation; DoD Instruction 8550.01, DoD Internet Services and Internet-Based Capabilities.

Principle Purpose

AFSAC Online hosts a suite of applications for Foreign Military Sales (FMS) Customers and to support the Security Cooperation Mission. All users accessing AFSAC Online are bound by the restrictions defined in SAAR form (DD2875).

Privacy Act

AFSAC Online data is protected under the Privacy Act of 1974. The applicable Privacy Act System of Records Notice (K890.15) is available at the Federal Register. Information regarding safeguarding PII can be found at the DoD Privacy Office.

Section 508 Accessibility

The U.S. Department of Defense is committed to making its electronic and information technologies accessible to individuals with disabilities in accordance with Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act (29 U.S.C. 794d), as amended in 1998.
