About AFSAC

The Air Force Security Assistance and Cooperation Directorate leads FMS program management and international partnership support on behalf of the U.S. Air Force.

Section 01

Our Mission

To provide professional security assistance and cooperation support that strengthens alliances, builds partner capacity, and enhances interoperability — advancing U.S. national security objectives through Foreign Military Sales.

AFSAC serves as the principal U.S. Air Force organization responsible for managing Foreign Military Sales (FMS) cases from cradle to grave. We coordinate the full spectrum of security cooperation activities — from case development and financial management to logistics, training, and sustainment support — for partner nations worldwide.

AFSAC Online is the digital portal through which authorized FMS customers and partner nation representatives access program data, submit requisitions, track shipments, report discrepancies, and monitor Case data — providing transparency and enabling informed decision-making across all security cooperation programs.

Section 02

What We Do

Case Management

End-to-end FMS case development, execution, financial tracking, and closeout for partner nation programs.

Logistics Support

Requisition processing, catalog management, supply chain visibility, and discrepancy resolution.

Partnership Development

Strengthening bilateral and multilateral relationships through collaborative security cooperation programs.

Data & Reporting

Automated metrics, performance dashboards, and transparent data access for authorized stakeholders.
